Linguistic Cache

About the App

Use the internet without the internet

We use Natural Language Processing and Computational Linguistics to predict what you would search based on your previous searches.

We then use our predicted phrases as a search key, then save the first website in the cache of your computer, allowing you to make searches without wifi.
